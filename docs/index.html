What is our aim?

We are getting tired of our big brother listening in on everything we do and selling our data. Our aim is to fight this so called 'big brother' by altering windows in such a way that our privacy is secured and lets us keep to ourselves.

What does Little Brother do?

Little Brother is a tool which edits the registry on your Windows machine, disabling certain settings such as sending usage data to Microsoft, allowing you to enjoy your time knowing that you are not being spied on by companies.

This application requires administrator access in order to work, and lets you choose which keys to edit, or you can simply run our recommended changes, letting you get on with your day quickly!

Who are we?

We are University students studying Cyber Security at the University of Derby with a passion for maintaining the privacy of the people, and creating great things.
